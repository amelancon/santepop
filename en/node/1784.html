The Global Health Research Capacity Strengthening Program (GHR-CAPS) of the Quebec Population Health Research Network (QPHRN) promotes global health research within Canada and internationally. It aims to strengthen the research capacity of our future leaders in global health by creating opportunities for learning, skill development and personal growth. The objectives of GHR-CAPS are to: (1) support the transition and integration of young researchers into the scientific community and (2) foster an interdisciplinary approach in global health research.

Ethical Challenges and Issues in Global Health Research

Save the Date: November 11, 2016 (10am-4pm).

Venue: Moot Court, Room 100, New Chancellor Day Hall, McGill University
(3644 Peel Street).

Join us for a full-day workshop on the ethical issues and challenges encountered in the conduct of global health research - featuring guest speakers, a panel and much more!

Event jointly organized by the SG Global Health and SG Ethics and Integrity of the QPHRN. More information to come soon.

For more information, please contact: Kariane St-Denis (GS Global Health): santemondiale-rrspq.med@mcgill.ca or Gabriella Kranz (SG Ethics): santemondiale-rrspq.med@mcgill.ca

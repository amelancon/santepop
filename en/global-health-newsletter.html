Global Health Newsletter


One of the objectives of the Global Health Network Axis is

to support the community of researchers in global health.



In recent years, a monthly newsletter was published in order to inform researchers/fellows/students about funding opportunities, jobs and events related to the field of global health. Previously submitted via email, the newsletter has received a complete makeover in 2011 and now provides a more visual and dynamic format.

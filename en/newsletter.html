PopHealth is an electronic newsletter that seeks to facilitate access to population health research with an eye to knowledge sharing.

PopHealth identifies events, recent publications (grey literature and publications of our members only - this is not a systematic review) and any other relevant information for population health researchers and players. This free publication keeps you abreast of what is new on the population health research portal. Do not hesitate to contact us if you have any information that could be of interest for our readers (more than 3000 so far): thierry.hurlimann@umontreal.ca
